Joseph

Genre: Thriller / Drama

Duration: 2h 18m

Release Year: 2018

Joseph, directed by M. Padmakumar, is a gripping Malayalam thriller that slowly unravels both a mystery and a man’s haunting past.

Joju George delivers a career-defining performance as Joseph, a retired police officer drawn into a personal investigation. His portrayal of a broken, sharp-minded man is both subtle and devastating.

The movie shines in how it blends emotion with investigation — a rare feat. Themes of loss, morality, and medical malpractice are weaved into a complex yet relatable narrative.

Joseph isn’t just a thriller; it’s a meditation on grief and human fragility. Thought-provoking and layered, it’s a gem in Malayalam cinema.
