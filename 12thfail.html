12th Fail

Genre: Biographical / Drama

Duration: 2h 26m

Release Year: 2023

12th Fail, directed by Vidhu Vinod Chopra, is a true underdog story that hits all the right notes — emotional, motivational, and grounded in reality.

Based on the life of IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma, the film follows a young man’s journey from rural adversity to the civil services. Vikrant Massey delivers a stunning performance filled with honesty and grit.

The movie is simple in treatment but powerful in message. It explores themes of failure, poverty, and resilience with an unflinching gaze.

In an era of larger-than-life heroes, 12th Fail reminds us of the strength in simplicity. A story that uplifts and inspires.
